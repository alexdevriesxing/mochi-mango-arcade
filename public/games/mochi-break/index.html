Mochi Break

Bounce a sticky rice ball through layers of wafer-thin mochi bricks. Mochi Breaker aims for perfect angles to crack through frozen matcha blocks and red bean barriers.

How to Play Mochi Break

Mochi Break is easy to pick up. Swipe or use arrow keys to move the paddle. Keep the ball in play and break all the bricks to win.

Tips & Strategy

Keep the ball on your paddle by moving to where it will land, not where it is. Aim for the top bricks first.
