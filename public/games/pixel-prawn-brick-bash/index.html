How to Play Pixel Prawn Brick Bash

Pixel Prawn Brick Bash is easy to pick up. Swipe or use arrow keys to move the paddle. Keep the ball in play and break all the bricks to win.

Tips & Strategy

Keep the ball on your paddle by moving to where it will land, not where it is. Aim for the top bricks first.
