Swap corrupted pixel plants into matching rows to restore a glitched garden before the data-scour wipes every bloom.

How to Play Glitch Garden

Glitch Garden is easy to pick up. Tap or click adjacent tiles to swap them. Match three or more identical tiles in a row to clear them and score points.

Tips & Strategy

Look for chains — clearing one match may create another. Focus on the bottom of the board to trigger cascading combos.
