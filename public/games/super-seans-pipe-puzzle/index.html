How to Play Super Sean's Pipe Puzzle

Super Sean's Pipe Puzzle is easy to pick up. Tap pipe segments to rotate them. Connect the water source to the endpoint by forming an unbroken path before time runs out.

Tips & Strategy

Work from the source outward — trace the path before making moves. Dead-end segments can be rotated back into useful positions.
