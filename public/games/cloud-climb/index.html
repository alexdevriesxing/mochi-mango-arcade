How to Play Cloud Climb

Cloud Climb is easy to pick up. Tilt your device or use arrow keys to move left and right. Keep jumping upward and don't fall off the screen!

Tips & Strategy

Land in the center of platforms for the most stable bounce. Move toward the side you want to go early.
