How to Play Mochi Match

Mochi Match is easy to pick up. Tap or click adjacent tiles to swap them. Match three or more identical tiles in a row to clear them and score points.

Tips & Strategy

Look for chains — clearing one match may create another. Focus on the bottom of the board to trigger cascading combos.
