How to Play Comet Quarry Crew

Comet Quarry Crew is easy to pick up. Rotate your ship with left/right, thrust forward with up, and fire with space or tap. Destroy floating asteroids before they hit you — they split into smaller pieces when shot.

Tips & Strategy

Smaller asteroids move faster but are worth more points. Keep moving — a stationary ship is an easy target. Aim for openings between asteroid clusters.
