How to Play Mochi Glide

Mochi Glide is easy to pick up. Tap or click repeatedly to flap and stay aloft. Time your taps carefully to navigate through gaps.

Tips & Strategy

Short, steady taps work better than long presses. Find a comfortable rhythm and stick to it.
