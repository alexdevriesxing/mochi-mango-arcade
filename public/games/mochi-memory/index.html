Flip mochi tiles to find matching pairs of traditional sweets in the Hall of Sweet Remembrance. Mochi Scholar tests your memory across increasingly complex boards of wagashi.

How to Play Mochi Memory

Mochi Memory is easy to pick up. Tap or click cards to flip them. Match pairs to clear the board. Remember where each card is hiding!

Tips & Strategy

Start from one corner and work systematically. Say each card aloud as you flip it to lock it in memory.
