How to Play Disk Drive Dash

Disk Drive Dash is easy to pick up. Move left or right with arrows, drag, or swipe. Collect falling goodies while avoiding hazardous objects. Each catch adds score — each hit costs a life.

Tips & Strategy

Watch the edges — hazards often come in clusters from one side. Prioritize power-ups over score items. Memorize spawn patterns to predict danger.
