Race through a neon-lit 80s suburb delivering mix tapes before sunset. Dodge sprinklers, barking dogs, and mailbox obstacles while the chiptune soundtrack builds speed. Every successful delivery unlocks a new cassette color.

How to Play Cassette Courier

Cassette Courier is easy to pick up. Tap or click to jump. Tap again while in the air to perform a double jump. Swipe down to slide under obstacles.

Tips & Strategy

Timing is everything — watch the obstacles and find the rhythm. Collect power-ups for temporary invincibility.
