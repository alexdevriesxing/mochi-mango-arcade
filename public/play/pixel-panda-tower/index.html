How to Play Pixel Panda Tower

Pixel Panda Tower is easy to pick up. Tap at the right moment to place each block. Stack them as evenly as possible for the tallest tower.

Watch the block's movement speed — it changes with tower height. A steady rhythm beats frantic tapping.
