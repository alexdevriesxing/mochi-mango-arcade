How to Play Alchemy Merge

Alchemy Merge is easy to pick up. Tap and drag identical items together to merge them into a more valuable item. Keep merging to discover rare items!

Tips & Strategy

Keep your board organized — grouped items are easier to merge. Don't merge too quickly; wait for the perfect match.
