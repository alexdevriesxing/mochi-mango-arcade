Lantern Mill

Place nine lanterns at ancient crossroads and form mills of three. Lantern Keeper guards the old ways in this elegant alignment game. Every line of three glowing lanterns removes a rival piece from the board.

How to Play Lantern Mill

Lantern Mill is easy to pick up. Tap or click on the board to select and move your pieces. Drag to rearrange, or tap again to confirm your move.

Tips & Strategy

Think ahead — plan your moves several turns in advance. Control the center of the board for maximum strategic advantage.
