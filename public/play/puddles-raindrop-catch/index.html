How to Play Puddle's Raindrop Catch

Puddle's Raindrop Catch is easy to pick up. Move left or right with arrows, drag, or swipe. Collect falling goodies while avoiding hazardous objects. Each catch adds score — each hit costs a life.

Watch the edges — hazards often come in clusters from one side. Prioritize power-ups over score items. Memorize spawn patterns to predict danger.
