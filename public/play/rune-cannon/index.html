How to Play Rune Cannon

Rune Cannon is easy to pick up. Tap or drag to aim your cannon. Release to fire. Match colors to clear groups and score combos.

Tips & Strategy

Plan several shots ahead. Bouncing shots off the walls can reach tricky spots.
