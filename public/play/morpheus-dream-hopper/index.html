How to Play Morpheus Dream Hopper

Morpheus Dream Hopper is easy to pick up. Tap or click to jump. Tap again while in the air to perform a double jump. Swipe down to slide under obstacles.

Tips & Strategy

Timing is everything — watch the obstacles and find the rhythm. Collect power-ups for temporary invincibility.
