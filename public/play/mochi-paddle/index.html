How to Play Mochi Paddle

Mochi Paddle is easy to pick up. Swipe or use arrow keys to move your paddle up and down. Block the ball and score past your opponent.

Tips & Strategy

Stay centered and react to the ball's angle. Try to return the ball to the edges for harder saves.
