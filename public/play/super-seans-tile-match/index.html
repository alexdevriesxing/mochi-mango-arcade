How to Play Super Sean's Tile Match

Super Sean's Tile Match is easy to pick up. Tap or click adjacent tiles to swap them. Match three or more identical tiles in a row to clear them and score points.

Look for chains — clearing one match may create another. Focus on the bottom of the board to trigger cascading combos.
