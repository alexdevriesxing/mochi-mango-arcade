How to Play The Tiny Titan Toy War

The Tiny Titan Toy War is easy to pick up. Tap to fire at targets. Drag to aim. Collect power-ups to upgrade your weapons and increase firepower.

Tips & Strategy

Lead your shots — aim where the target is going, not where it is. Conserve power-ups for tough waves.
