How to Play Trawler's Catch

Trawler's Catch is easy to pick up. Tap to cast your line, then wait for a bite. Tap again to reel in your catch. Time your pulls carefully!

Tips & Strategy

Watch for the bobber to dip below the surface — that's when you strike. Different fish prefer different depths.
