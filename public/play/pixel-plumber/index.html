Rotate and connect leaking pixel pipes before the basement floods. Each dripping joint and pressure gauge adds challenge as Piper Penguin races against the rising water in this retro pipe-dream puzzle.

How to Play Pixel Plumber

Pixel Plumber is easy to pick up. Tap pipe segments to rotate them. Connect the water source to the endpoint by forming an unbroken path before time runs out.

Tips & Strategy

Work from the source outward — trace the path before making moves. Dead-end segments can be rotated back into useful positions.
