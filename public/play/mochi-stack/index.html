How to Play Mochi Stack

Mochi Stack is easy to pick up. Tap or click to stop the moving platform. Each successful stack adds a new layer. Precision is key!

Tips & Strategy

Watch the speed pattern — it follows a rhythm. Aim for the center of each platform for the most stable stack.
