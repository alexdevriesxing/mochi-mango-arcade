# Production Asset Manifest

## Runtime sprites

- `assets/sprites/petroman-spritesheet.png` — illustrated Petroman animation frames
- `assets/sprites/enemy-spritesheet.png` — four enemy families and Smog King frames
- `assets/sprites/purifier-bomb-atlas.png` — four-frame Purifier Charge animation
- `assets/sprites/pickups-atlas.png` — upgrade gems, reactor orb, and PETRO letters
- `assets/sprites/vfx-spritesheet.png` — illustrated explosion, impact, ring, and collection effects
- `assets/sprites/object-atlas-source.png` — approved illustrated object source atlas

## Tiles and environments

- `assets/tiles/world-tiles.png` — six world rows containing floor, hard wall, destructible block, hazard, conveyor, ice, teleport, and exit art
- `assets/backgrounds/world-1.png` through `world-6.png` — painted environment backdrops

## UI and key art

- Illustrated title, stage-clear, pause, victory, and defeat screens
- Illustrated HUD frame, side panel, and icon atlas
- 1600×900 hero, gameplay, victory, and defeat key art
- 800×450 and 512×288 thumbnails
- 512×512 application icon

## Audio

- Title, six world, victory, and defeat music tracks
- Bomb placement, explosion, kick, power-up, collection, damage, clear, start, life, and enemy SFX

## Full-resolution source art

The approved high-resolution hand-illustrated source sheets used to produce the runtime atlases are included under `assets/source-art`.
